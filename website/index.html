Our Story

The Problem We're Solving

There is not a single spice brand in India truly known for authenticity and purity. Everyday, news of adulteration shakes consumer trust. Even premium brands fail food safety tests. Industrial machines generate extreme heat that destroys essential oils, flavour, and nutrition.

"We don't just sell spices. We revive a forgotten craft that makes food the way it was meant to taste."

At Ration Veda, we revive the age-old hand-pounding technique using the Imam-Dasta (mortar & pestle). This low-heat, slow process preserves 100% of the essential oils and natural potency — delivering flavour that machines simply cannot replicate.

Every spice is sourced directly from the region known for the finest harvest of that particular ingredient. No middlemen. No compromise.

Our Impact

More Than Spices.
A Movement.
