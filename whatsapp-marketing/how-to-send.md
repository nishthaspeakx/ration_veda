**Step 2: Send the short text message**

## Forwarding tips

### ✅ DO
- **Send poster FIRST, text SECOND** — image gets attention, text makes the ask clear
- **Use WhatsApp Business app** (not personal) — shows your business name + greetings
- **Reply within 1 hour** — WhatsApp orders go cold fast
- **Save the poster to your phone's Photos** so it's easy to re-send
- **Post the poster as WhatsApp Status daily** for the first 2 weeks
- **Personalize** the first line when sending to a group: "Hi [Group Name]! 🌿"

### ❌ DON'T
- Don't send in groups where forwarding is not allowed (check group rules)
- Don't send more than once every 2 weeks to the same group
- Don't forward with "Forwarded many times" tag — retype/copy fresh
- Don't send in 20 groups at once — spread over a few days (WhatsApp flags spam)

## Auto-reply setup (WhatsApp Business)

In **WhatsApp Business → Settings → Business tools → Greeting message**, set:

```
🌿 Hi! Welcome to Ration Veda — Hand-Pounded Premium Spices.

Aap ne sahi jagah message kiya! 💛

Hamare paas 4 hero spices hain:
🌶️ Guntur Chilli — ₹100
🌿 Rajasthani Dhaniya — ₹80
🟡 Rajapuri Haldi — ₹100
🖤 Punjabi Garam Masala — ₹200

Order karna hai? Bas product name type kariye aur hum arrange kar denge.

Delivery pan-India available. 📦
```

Also set **Away message** for after-hours:

```
Thank you for your message! 🌿
Hum abhi available nahi hain, but will get back to you within 4 hours.
Meanwhile — aap order type kar sakte hain, hum confirm karenge. 💛
```
