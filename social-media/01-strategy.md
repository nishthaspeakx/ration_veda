# 01 — Social Media Strategy

## Brand Positioning (one line)
> *"Ration Veda revives the forgotten craft of hand-pounded spices — pure, region-sourced, and made by the hands of rural women."*

## Why this works
- **Authenticity** (hand-pounded Imam-Dasta) → differentiates from every mass-market brand
- **Origin-specific** (Guntur, Rajasthan, Rajapuri, Punjab) → premium trust signal
- **Purpose** (women farmers) → emotional hook + shareability

## Voice & Tone
| Tone | Do | Don't |
|------|----|----|
| **Warm, grounded, proud** | "Ground by hand. Tasted by heart." | ❌ "Best spices in India!!!" |
| **Sensory** | "The aroma of sun-dried Guntur chillies being pounded at dawn…" | ❌ Generic adjectives |
| **Story-first** | Every post centers a human, a place, or a moment | ❌ Feature lists |

**3 words to guide every caption:** *Pure. Ancient. Honest.*

## The 8 Content Pillars (rotated through the month)

| # | Pillar | % of posts | Purpose |
|---|--------|-----------|---------|
| 1 | **Product Showcase** — the 4 spices, solo hero shots | 15% | Desire |
| 2 | **Imam-Dasta Process** — hand-pounding, slow craft | 15% | Differentiation |

## Platform Strategy

### Instagram (primary)
- **Reels**: 5–6/week (hero content, Higgsfield-made)
- **Carousels**: 2/week (recipes, process breakdown, origin stories)
- **Stories daily**: BTS, polls, countdowns, UGC reposts
- **Highlights**: Process / Products / Origins / Events / Gifting / Reviews

### YouTube Shorts
- Upload every Reel here (vertical, <60s)
- Captions heavy in keywords: *hand pounded spices, imam dasta, organic garam masala*

### Facebook
- Cross-post Reels + carousels
- Longer captions work here — tell the full story
- Join "Delhi/NCR foodies" and "Organic food India" groups; share 1x/week

### LinkedIn
- 3x/week — founder's voice
- Angles: *building a women-led supply chain*, *reviving ancient craft as a business*, *D2C premium food learnings*
- Drives B2B corporate gifting leads

### X (Twitter)
- Daily 1–2 posts: quote cards, quick threads ("Why hand-pounded chilli tastes different — a thread 🌶️🧵")
- Engage with food writers, chefs, restaurant accounts
