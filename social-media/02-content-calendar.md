## Cross-platform adaptation rules

**Instagram (master):** Full caption, all hashtags, Reel/carousel as-is.

**YouTube Shorts:** Same video. Title = first line of caption + "| Ration Veda". Description includes order link + 10 hashtags.

**Facebook:** Same video. Keep caption **longer** — add a paragraph of story. Minimal hashtags (3–5).

**LinkedIn (Mon/Wed/Fri only):** Re-angle for professional audience:
- Drop emojis to 1–2 max
- Lead with a business/impact insight
- End with a soft ask (partnership, B2B gifting, thoughts)

**X (Twitter):** Compress to <240 chars. Use video if <2m20s. Thread format for carousels.

## Stories schedule (daily, don't skip!)
- **Mon/Thu**: BTS (packing, pounding, delivery)
- **Tue/Fri**: Polls / Questions ("Guntur or Kashmiri chilli?")
- **Wed/Sat**: UGC repost + "add yours"
- **Sun**: Week recap + Monday teaser
