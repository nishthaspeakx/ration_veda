# 06 — Higgsfield Setup Guide

## What is Higgsfield?
Higgsfield is an AI video generation platform that turns text prompts into cinematic short video clips (3–10 seconds). It's one of the best tools currently available for **social-media-ready vertical video** with realistic motion and cinematic quality.

Website: https://higgsfield.ai

---

## Plan Recommendation for Ration Veda

You need **20 videos per month**, each typically 5–8 seconds. Some concepts (V19 brand film) need 4 stitched clips = up to 30 generations when counting retries.

**Realistic monthly credit need:** 60–80 generations (assuming 2–3 takes per video to pick the best).

| Plan | Price (₹) | Credits/mo | Videos/mo (realistic) | Good for you? |
|------|----------|-----------|----------------------|---------------|
| **Lite** | ~₹800 (~$9) | 150 | 5–8 | ❌ Too few credits |
| **Creator** | ~₹2,500 (~$29) | 450 | 20–30 | ✅ **Recommended for Month 1** |
| **Pro** | ~₹6,500 (~$79) | 1500 | 60+ | Upgrade once scaling to daily videos |

> ⚠️ Prices change — check Higgsfield's current pricing page before subscribing.

### 👉 Start with **Creator plan**
This covers your 20 videos/month plus retries, with enough headroom to experiment.

## Step-by-step: Generate your first video

1. **Sign up** at https://higgsfield.ai → subscribe to Creator plan
2. **Go to "Create"** → choose **"Text to Video"**
3. **Aspect ratio:** select **9:16** (vertical)
4. **Duration:** **8 seconds** (max on Creator)
5. **Style preset:** try **"Cinematic"** first
6. **Motion:** **"Standard"** for most prompts; **"Strong"** for dynamic reels; **"Subtle"** for product hero shots
7. **Paste the prompt** from `03-higgsfield-video-prompts.md`
8. **Generate** → wait ~60 seconds
9. **Review** — if output looks distorted or misses the mood, **regenerate** (it's stochastic)
10. **Download** as MP4

## Post-production in CapCut (free)

After downloading from Higgsfield:

1. **Open CapCut** → new project
2. **Import** the Higgsfield clip(s)
3. **Add text overlays** — product names, subtitles, CTAs
4. **Add brand logo** (bottom-right corner, 50% opacity)
5. **Color grade** — apply a consistent LUT (use CapCut's "Warm Film" preset)
6. **Add music** — either:
   - Trending IG audio (check weekly in Reels → Audio → Trending)
   - Royalty-free Indian instrumental from Epidemic Sound / Artlist
7. **Add voiceover** (optional) — record in-app or import from ElevenLabs
8. **Captions** — CapCut's auto-caption feature, style bold + bottom-center
9. **Export** at **1080×1920**, **30fps**

## Consistent brand look — build ONE LUT file

To make all 20 videos feel like one brand, export this **color preset** from CapCut:

- **Exposure:** -5
- **Contrast:** +10
- **Saturation:** +8
- **Warmth:** +15
- **Tint:** +3 (slight magenta)
- **Highlights:** -8
- **Shadows:** +5
- **Grain:** +5

Save as a **"Ration Veda Warm"** preset. Apply to every video.

## Common issues & fixes

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Distorted hands / extra fingers | Regenerate 2–3x, or crop tighter to hide hands |
| Misspelled text on labels | Add text in CapCut post-gen, never rely on AI text |
| Spices look fake / plastic | Change prompt to "macro slow-motion, real physics, cinematic food photography" |
| Motion too jittery | Lower motion intensity or enable "Smooth motion" |
| Clip too short for story | Generate 2 clips, stitch in CapCut with crossfade |
| People look uncanny | Use wide shots / silhouettes / focus on hands; avoid close-up faces |

## Monthly Higgsfield workflow (1 day/month)

**Set aside one "video generation day" — usually the last Sunday of the month:**

1. Open `03-higgsfield-video-prompts.md`
2. Generate all 20 videos back-to-back (takes ~3 hours including retries)
3. Download all into `social-media/monthly-videos/[Month-Year]/` folder
4. Spend another 2–3 hours in CapCut adding text/music/grading
5. Upload all finished videos to a Google Drive folder named by week
6. Schedule via Meta Business Suite / Hootsuite throughout the month

**Total time for the whole month's video content: ~6 hours.**
